Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money really 100% protected?

Yes, at maturity you are guaranteed to receive back 100% of your total premiums paid, regardless of market performance.

How are the returns calculated?

Returns are based on the performance of a carefully selected basket of global market indices. We use a participation rate to determine how much of the index growth you receive.

What if I need to withdraw early?

Early withdrawals are possible but it will impact the final maturity value, especially in the first few years. We recommend this plan for money you can commit for the full term. For emergency funds, consider keeping those separate.
